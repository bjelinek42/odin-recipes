Spagetti

Directions

It's spagetti. Do I really need to say more?? Okay fine.

Ingredients

  • Spagetti Noodles
  • Spagetti Sauce
  • Ground Beef (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles per instruction on the package.
  2. Heat up spagetti sauce.
  3. Cook meat per instructions, or check out instructions here.
  4. When noodles are done, add beef and sauce.
  5. Eat!
