Soup

Directions

Tortilla with cheese, meat, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Cook meat
  2. Warm up tortilla in oven
  3. Schmear sour cream in a thin layer on tortilla
  4. Add meat, cheese, and veggies to tortilla
  5. Add salsa
  6. Fold and Eat!
