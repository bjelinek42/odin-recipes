Soup

Directions

Throw stuff in a pot with liquid and add heat for hours. Need more? Fine.

Ingredients

  • Broth
  • Meat
  • Veggies
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Heat up broth to boil
  2. Cook meat until done
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients.
  4. Cook for something like 2-4 hours or right before all the veggies become too mushy
  5. Eat!
